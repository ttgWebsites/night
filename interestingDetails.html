Interesting Details

  • Wiesel initially struggled with publishing his story, as many publishers viewed it as too frightening to read, however Wiesel pressed on, as the story was very important to tell. Today the book has been translated to over 30 languages, proving that despite the terrible story, it was a very important work of literature.
  • Night is actually the first book of a trilogy, this is not often known, as this is the most often read and taught book of the trilogy. The other two books go into more details of Wiesel's life after the holocaust.
  • Wiesel is still alive today, at 84, and still works hard to get his story out to the public.

Characters

  • Elie Wiesel
  • The protagonist of the novel and narrator
  • As a young boy Elie is sent to a concentration camp with his family during World War II due to their Jewish faith
  • Suffers many atrocities during the experience yet survives and makes it out of the camp
  • Chlomo Wiesel
  • Elie's father
  • Chlomo makes it to the concentration camp with Elie and his family and is separated with Elie from the rest of the family
  • After surviving nearly the whole concentration experience he dies
  • Mrs. Wiesel
  • Elie's kind and quiet mother
  • Once arriving at the camp with the family and her daughter she and the daughters are separated from Elie and Chlomo
  • Immediately sent to the gas chambers where her and her daughter are killed

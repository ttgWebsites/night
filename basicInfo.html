Basic Info

  • Title
  • Night
  • Author
  • Elie Wiesel
  • Published
  • First published in 1956 in Buenos Aires, and later translated and published in 1960 in the United States by Hill & Wang.
  • Genre
  • Autobiography, World War 2, Holocaust.
  • Setting
  • The novel is set during the years 1941 to 1945 and begins in Elie's home of Romania and then shifts throughout Poland and Germany as he spends time living in various concentration camps.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is told in the first person from the author's point of view.
  • Conflict
  • The main conflict in the novel deals with Elie's struggles during the holocaust as he and his family face the tortures of a Nazi concentration camp. It also deals with Elie's struggle with his own faith due to what he witnesses in the camps.
